What is Live Streaming?

and platforms to broadcast video and sound of an event over the internet

in real-time. Their viewers can interact with what they desire and problems,

and demand entertainment from the streamer's content. Gaming is

Why Do People Watch Streams?

Here are the reasons and benefits of why people watch streams nowadays:

Knowledge and Fun

People prefer basic and understandable information that the streamer presents to their content. However, they also want a fun vibe that the streamer can use to distract them from their boredom. Influencers today have a specific streaming pattern to retain and increase their current viewers.

Discover

The need for skills and knowledge makes the viewer search for streaming content that they want to develop or adapt to their habits and lifestyle. It is one of the reasons why people search for live streams due to their real-time interaction with the viewers and streamers without showing their privacy.

Community

Streaming has included social interaction, distinguishing it from other forms of media content. Due to the popularity of streaming, people now recognize their favorite streamers or content creators, which gave way to people’s success.
